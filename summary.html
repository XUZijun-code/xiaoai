Actionable Job Interview Advice

Key Steps for Interview Success

  • Research the employer and job title
  • Prepare and rehearse answers to common and job-specific questions
  • Arrive early and dress appropriately
  • Bring necessary materials (resume, portfolio, pen, paper)
  • Be respectful and build rapport with everyone
  • Use positive body language and nonverbal cues
  • Apply the STAR method for behavioral questions
  • Listen carefully and respond thoughtfully
  • Ask insightful questions about the role/company
  • Thank the interviewer and follow up after the interview
  • Review your performance and improve for next time
