# Top 5 Articles on Job Interview Skills

Below are five recommended articles about job interview skills, each with a link and a brief summary.

---

1. **[Top Interview Skills for Job Seekers](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/interviewing/interview-skills)**
   - Summary: Covers essential interview skills such as communication, preparation, confidence, and follow-up. Offers actionable tips for before, during, and after the interview.

2. **[Top Interview Tips: Common Questions, Body Language & More](https://www.thebalancemoney.com/top-interview-tips-2058577)**
   - Summary: Provides a comprehensive list of interview tips, including how to answer common questions, dress appropriately, and use positive body language.

3. **[Interview Skills: What They Are and How to Improve Them](https://www.careercontessa.com/advice/interview-skills/)**
   - Summary: Explains key interview skills, how to develop them, and strategies for showcasing them during interviews. Includes advice for both in-person and virtual interviews.

4. **[Interview Tips: How to Make a Great Impression](https://www.glassdoor.com/blog/guide/interview-tips/)**
   - Summary: Focuses on making a strong first impression, researching the company, and preparing thoughtful questions. Highlights the importance of follow-up.

5. **[How to Improve Your Interview Skills](https://hbr.org/2022/07/how-to-improve-your-interview-skills)**
   - Summary: Offers research-backed advice on improving interview performance, including practicing answers, managing nerves, and learning from feedback.

---

These articles provide a well-rounded overview of the most important job interview skills and actionable advice for job seekers.
